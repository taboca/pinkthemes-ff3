Search Engine got Pink

To access the pink search engine you can simply press CTRL+K ( or command K in your Mac ) and type your query, or click in the right hand corner search box. The custom PinkTheme search uses Google Search Web service and does not modify results. To change the theme style you can click the little heart icon located in the pink search results page at the right hand corner.
